Build your ideas on weekends. Turn projects into products. Shape your future in 48 hours per week.

What is Project Weekender?

Project Weekender is a mission for ambitious individuals who want to use their weekends intentionally. We build ideas, launch small products, test experiments, and share the journey publicly through a weekly newsletter. It’s about execution over excuses.

Why Weekends?

Your weekends are an unfair advantage. When most people disconnect, you build momentum. In just 48 hours per week, you can design, automate, launch, and validate real products that move your life forward.

The Long Game

As the community grows, Project Weekender evolves into something bigger—eventually a micro‑VC fund supporting weekend‑built startups. Builders backing builders.
